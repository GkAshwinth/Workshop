Top Selling Items In Store

Valorant

Valorant, developed by Riot Games, is a free-to-play tactical first-person shooter that has taken the gaming world by storm since its release in 2020. Combining precise gunplay with unique character abilities, the game delivers a dynamic and competitive experience.

Valorant's vibrant community and rapidly growing esports scene have cemented its place as a global gaming phenomenon. Riot Games continuously supports the game with regular updates, new Agents, maps, and seasonal battle passes, keeping the gameplay fresh and engaging. Its professional circuit, the Valorant Champions Tour (VCT), has attracted top-tier talent and a massive audience worldwide, offering thrilling competitions and showcasing the game's high skill ceiling

F1

The F1 game series, developed by Codemasters, delivers an authentic and thrilling Formula 1 racing experience to fans worldwide. With official licenses from the FIA Formula One World Championship, the game allows players to compete on iconic circuits, drive real-world F1 cars, and race as their favorite drivers or teams.

The F1 game stands out for its versatility, featuring multiple game modes like Career Mode, My Team, and online multiplayer, catering to a wide range of players. Career Mode immerses fans in the life of an F1 driver, from managing contracts to racing through seasons, while My Team allows players to build and lead their own F1 team.

FC 25

FC 25, the latest entry in the football gaming franchise, delivers a revolutionary experience that sets a new benchmark for realism and gameplay innovation. With cutting-edge graphics, lifelike animations, and advanced player intelligence, FC 25 captures the essence of the beautiful game like never before.

FC 25 introduces exciting new modes and updates, including a deeper Career Mode, where players can manage their club's journey to glory or create their own football legacy. Ultimate Team receives upgrades with new customization options and challenges, allowing players to build their dream squads.
